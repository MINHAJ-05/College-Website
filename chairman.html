Chairman's Message

Dear Students,

Laxman Devram Sonawane College was established in the year 2002 with the aim to impart quality and value based education to the young minds. Our objective is not only to nurture intellectual power but also to develop confidence which would lead to holistic personality development of the students. More than 5500 students get admission every year, ranging from Jr. College to P. G. Programmes, in diverse streams of excellence.

Our college is situated in the prime location of Kalyan and is well equipped with modern amenities such as spacious classrooms, state of the art Science & Computer laboratories, and a rich library with separate reading rooms for boys & girls. The college also prides itself with a playground that facilitates outdoor sports. Students are also encouraged to participate in sports and other extra-curricular activities. The canteen facility provides much needed refreshments to one and all. We empower our students with additional skills and prepare them to face the challenges in the world outside with courage and conviction. It gives me immense pride to share that our college has recently been accredited by National Accreditation & Assessment Council (NAAC) with “B” Grade. Furthermore receiving permanent affiliation from the University at Mumbai has been a great achievement for our college. The ISO Certification has been is yet another feather in the cap for the college.

Our college has been instrumental in shaping lives and mentoring minds year after year. It fills me with immense joy and pride to see thousands of students pursuing education at our college every year, who later progress towards becoming successful citizens and true ambassadors of the nation.

ABOUT US

We are committed to provide Quality Education with a mission to strengthen the minds and expand intellect of our students and to empower them with additional skills so that they could accept and face the challenges of 21st century and also accept the social responsibilities.
